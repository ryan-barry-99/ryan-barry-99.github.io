About Me

Robotics Research Engineer with an MS in Electrical Engineering from RIT, specializing in robotics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

I’m an engineer focused on designing robust robotic systems across embedded hardware, motion control, and electromechanical integration. My academic work at RIT emphasized robotics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, supported by hands-on experience in multidisciplinary teams.

At RIT, I contributed to the Electric Vehicle Team and the University Rover Challenge Team, building a strong foundation in C/C++, PCB design, Python, and system-level robotics development.

I’m currently a Research Engineer II at the Robotics and Automation Design Lab at Texas A&M University, where I support hardware and software development for robotic manipulators intended for use in spaceflight and mission-critical environments.
